About Us

Founded Fall 2023

Overview

No. 1 Buttons is a Grade 9 business class summative project. We are a 4 student business in Ottawa, Ontario. Each of us has a role:


VP of Design, CDO, CEO: Designs and develops everything including buttons, website, online button making tool, and more. Also helps everyone else in the group.
VP of Marketing, CMO: Advertising and selling buttons, as well as promoting our social media.
VP of Production, Button maker: Manually makes every single button.
VP of Finance, CFO: Tracks things like profits and revenue through spreadsheet.
